My Favorite Hobbies
Reading, Dancing, Coding, Building, Working Out, and Playing Games

As you may have guessed from the title, I have a lot of hobbies. I couldn't try and choose one, but I decided to talk about all of the ones I like the most. I like reading, because I learn so much from books, and the stories are combined in my mind to create one big story. I write them down, and they actually boost my creatvity up. I wonder if any of the stories I write will ever get published, and that's what keeps me going when it comes to writing books(well, not actual books, although I do like to write as much as I can). Anyways, if there were no books to read, we would never be as educated as we are now. There wouldn't be a trusted way of spreading information. All of that means that books can not only help my writing skills, but we get a better understanding of the world in the author's point of view.

Something else that I like is dancing, because it not only helps me get active, but it also makes me happy. The only bad thing is when I am in a bad mood, I don't feel like dancing unless I put on a funny song. Nevertheless, dancing is my favorite. If I had to judge what was good before I knew about building and coding, I would have included only reading and dancing.

Coding is another one of my hobbies that I just started recently, early this year. I like coding because it has introduced me to a new perspcetive of things I can like. I never used to like games so much, and it wouldn't have been on the list had I not learned coding and realized how hard it must be for people who make so many games and movies out of code. It takes a lot of time and I won't ever take these games and movies for granted anymore.

I like building because just like coding, I can think out of the box. I mainly build with legos, but am ready to build with anything as long as I have the permission and materials to do it.

I like working out because it helps me shed out some extra fat, and I gained a LOT of fat this year not being able to do much. It also helps tire me out, and I can sleep much better that way.

I like playing just about any game(although I still do have a few exceptions when it comes to that). I'll explain. If you ask me to play an action game, I'm all up for it. An arcade game, not such a big fan. I can still play any game if it's fun, though, or if it includes any of the given hobbies above.
